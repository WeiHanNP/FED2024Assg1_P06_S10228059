Awards

Mayday (五月天) is a highly acclaimed Taiwanese rock band formed in the late 1990s. Known for their blend of rock and mandopop, the group includes five members: Ashin (vocals), Monster (guitar), Stone (guitar), Masa (bass), and Guan You (drums). Their music often resonates with themes of youth and life, making them iconic figures in Asian music. The band has won over 150 music awards worldwide, including notable honors like 12 Golden Melody Awards, which are often considered the Grammys of Mandarin music.

Their accolades include "Best Band" awards and recognition for their songwriting, albums, and music videos. In 2012, their album Second Round was particularly successful, earning them six Golden Melody Awards​ They've also achieved remarkable global milestones, such as being the first Chinese band to perform at Madison Square Garden and staging sold-out concerts worldwide, including venues in Las Vegas and Beijing

The band has won over 12 Golden Melody Awards, including four for "Best Band" (2001, 2004, 2009, 2012). Their album Second Round won six awards in 2012, including Best Mandarin Album, Song of the Year, and Producer of the Year. They've also received the MTV Video Music Award Japan's Best Buzz Asia Award
