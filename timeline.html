Timeline

1999

Mayday was formed in 1999 when Ashin, Monster, Stone, Masa, and Guan You were studying at The Affiliated Senior High School of National Taiwan Normal University. The members of Mayday were childhood friends and came from the same elementary school. They were initially called So Band, but after discovering a band in Japan with the same name, they changed the name to Mayday, which means "help" in Chinese. The band performed in pubs and released an album in 2000.

2000

Released multiple hit albums, including "Viva Love" (愛情萬歲) and "People Life, Ocean Wild" (人生海海). Known for heartfelt lyrics and energetic performances that resonated with young audiences.

2004

The band took a brief break as members pursued individual projects. They returned with the album "Time Machine" (神的遊戲) in 2004 and reestablished their popularity.

2006

Released the album "Born to Love" (為愛而生) and embarked on large-scale tours across Asia. Their concert "Jump! The World Tour" solidified them as international stars.

2011

Albums like "Second Round" (第二人生) and "History of Tomorrow" (自傳) brought critical acclaim. Played at major venues, including the Tokyo Dome and Madison Square Garden.

2018

Continued releasing music and touring worldwide, including the "Mayday Life Tour" (五月天人生無限公司巡演). Produced and starred in the 2019 concert film "Mayday Life", blending their music with cinematic storytelling.
