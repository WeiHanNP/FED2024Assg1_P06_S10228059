Mayday's First Album

Mayday's first album was released in 1999. It was called "Mayday's First Album". The album was a huge success and it was the start of Mayday's journey to become one of the most popular bands in Taiwan. The album was a mix of rock, pop, and ballads. It was well received by the public and critics alike. The album was a commercial success and it sold over 100,000 copies in Taiwan alone. The album was a critical success as well. It was praised for its songwriting, musicianship, and production. The album was nominated for several awards and it won the Best New Artist award at the Golden Melody Awards. The album was a huge success and it established Mayday as one of the most popular bands in Taiwan.
